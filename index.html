2. 12-Initiative Implementation Timeline

A sequential roadmap for delivering K2's custom features, organized by phase for clear operational delivery.

1 Phase 1: Foundation
Weeks 1 - 14 • 7 Sprints
1. Projects & Rooms Sprints 1-2

Centralized admin dashboard organizing renovation jobs room-by-room with instant status monitors.

2. Tasks & Daily Reports Sprints 3-4

Speech-to-text site logging for field managers to dictate updates and auto-generate checklists.

3. Plans & Documents Sprints 5-6

Cloud drawing catalog and version control ensuring crews always build from the latest approved blueprint.

4. Materials List Sprint 7

Interactive shopping selections tracking responsibilities, suppliers, and arrival dates on site.

2 Phase 2: Operations
Weeks 15 - 24 • 5 Sprints
5. Schedule Engine Sprints 8-9

Interactive Gantt timeline linked to active tasks that auto-alerts sub trades if schedule dates shift.

6. Estimates Builder Sprint 10

Bespoke bidding worksheets featuring preset square-foot trade rates to speed up client quotes.

7. Budgets & Allowances Sprint 11

Real-time ledger tracking client allowance balances, payments made, and upgrade deltas.

8. Invoice Ingestion Sprint 12

Automated subcontractor billing email parser routing parsed invoices for PM office approvals.

3 Phase 3: Intelligence
Weeks 25 - 36 • 6 Sprints
9. Project Chat Threads Sprint 13

Secure, searchable channels dedicated to individual renovation jobs, replacing text trails.

10. Homeowner Portal Sprints 14-15

Premium customer workspace highlighting progress timelines, site photos, and sign-offs.

11. AI Project Assistant Sprints 16-17

Natural-language assistant answering queries regarding blueprints and design selections.

12. Scorecards & KPI Sprint 18

KPI metrics tracking subcontractor schedule adherence and superintendent blocker logs.
